FAQ

Common Questions

How much does an interior design project cost? +

Our projects vary widely depending on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically begin from ₹15 lakhs, while commercial projects start from ₹30 lakhs. We offer transparent, fixed-fee proposals so there are no surprises.

How long does a project typically take? +

A 2-bedroom apartment renovation typically takes 4–6 months from first consultation to final installation. Larger homes and commercial projects can take 8–12 months. We provide a detailed timeline at the start of every project.

Do you work outside Mumbai? +

We are based in Davanagere and Channagiri and serve clients across Karnataka. For projects further afield, we build travel costs into the project proposal. Contact us to discuss your location.

Can I be involved in the design process? +

Absolutely — we encourage it. Our process is deeply collaborative. You'll review and approve every concept phase before we move forward, ensuring the result is always a true reflection of your vision.
